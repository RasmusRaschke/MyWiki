``` ad-Proposition
title: Proposition ($\cC^\infty$ is an [[algebra]]).

Let $\cC^\infty (M, \R)$ be the [[vector space]] of smooth functions from a [[manifold|smooth manifolds]] $M$ to $\R$. Pointwise multiplication makes $\cC^\infty (M, \R)$ an [[algebra]] over $\R$.
```

<i>Proof.</i> Trivial.
