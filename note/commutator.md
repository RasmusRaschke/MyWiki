``` ad-Definition
title: Definition (Commutator).

Let $\cD_1, \cD_2 \in \text{Der}(V)$ with a [[vector space]] $V$. The __commutator__ is given by $$[\cD_1, \cD_2] := \cD_1 \cD_2 - \cD_2 \cD_1.$$

```
**Remark.**
The commutator is again a derivation.
