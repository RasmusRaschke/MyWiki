# Of Topological Spaces

``` ad-Definition
title: Definition (Cone).
Let $X$ be a [[topological space]]. The <ins>cone</ins> $CX$ of $X$ is the quotient
$$
CX := (X \times [0,1])/(X \times \{0\}).
$$

```
