``` ad-Definition
title: Definition (Grassman Manifolds).

The $k$ dimensional subspaces $G_{k,n}$ of $\R^n$ are called __Grassman manifolds__.

```
**Remark.**
They gain a smooth structure analogous to the [[projective spaces|real projective space]].
