## Diameter

``` ad-Definition
title: Definition (Diameter).
Let $A \sub X$ be a subset and $(X,d)$ be a metric space. The <ins>diameter</ins> of $A$ is given by
$$
\diam(A) := \sup \{d(x,y) \mid x,y \in A\}.
$$

```
