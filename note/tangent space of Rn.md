``` ad-Definition
title: Definition (Tangent Space in $\R^n$).

The __tangent space__ $T_p\R^n$ at a point $p \in \R^n$ is given by $$T_p\R^n:= \{(p, \xi) | \xi \in \R^n \}.$$
```
